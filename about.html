Who we are

My wife and I are looking forward to the summer holidays this year.

We are both very enthusiastic cyclists. This year we are planning a cycling holiday in northern Norway. Specifically in Nordland county.

I have a few years left before I retire. My wife is already retired. She has worked as an anesthesia nurse.

Fortunately, we are both physically fit, and therefore look forward to a cycling holiday along the coast of Nordland.

As you may have already guessed, this blog will be about planning our cycling holiday in Nordland.

What we will do in the summer holidays

We will spend the night in our own tent and in hotels, depending on what suits us best.

We hope for nice days with sunshine, but are also prepared for rain, and will dress as the weather requires.

When cycling, you pay more attention to nature and the landscape than when driving a car.

How we have planned the holiday

We plan daily cycling stages in the region around the towns of Brønnøysund and Sandnessjøen.

The whole holiday trip will take about 2 weeks.

First we will need a couple of days to drive from our hometown in Krokkleiva to the town of Brønnøysund. The drive will take 13 hours by car. It is about 900 kilometers to drive.

In Sandnessjøen we will rent bikes and take the ferry to the island Vega. We look forward to a bike ride around the whole of Vega. This island is located right by the sea and the cycling terrain is very flat.

We will cycle using GPS and physical maps.

Why would we spend our holiday this way?

Cycling will give us a lot of fresh air, which is good for health.

Such a holiday would be to do something completely different than we do on a daily basis. It will be a good relaxation and give us a lot of extra energy.

We will have many shared memories and experiences that we can think back on with joy.
